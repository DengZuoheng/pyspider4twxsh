title: 我院师生勇夺学校新生合唱比赛一等奖
date: 2011-10-16
content: 
<p style="TEXT-JUSTIFY: inter-ideograph; TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 18pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US">2011</span><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US">10</span><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US">15</span><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日晚七点，暨南大学新生校歌合唱比赛于珠海校区体育馆拉开帷幕。经过一个多月的精心筹备和训练，我院凭着极具特色的表演和嘹亮的歌声一举夺得一等奖、优秀组织奖、最佳创意奖三项大奖。</span></font></p><p style="TEXT-JUSTIFY: inter-ideograph; TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 18pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align="center"><font size="3"><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"></span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US"><?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><p><img border="0" src="/twxsh/upload/2012/4/2.jpg"></p></span></font></p><font size="3"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US"><p>
<p style="TEXT-JUSTIFY: inter-ideograph; TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 18pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">本次比赛中，来自四个校区的</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US">20</span><span style="COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">支参赛队伍通过乐诗朗诵、武术、舞蹈等各种形式展示了各自的风采。面对来自全校各学院强劲的参赛队伍，我院团委学生会秉持着严谨、高效、认真负责的原则，力求将节目的每个细节做到最好。</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: Calibri; COLOR: windowtext; mso-font-kerning: 1.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'" lang="EN-US"><p></p></span></font></p>
