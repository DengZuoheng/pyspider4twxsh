title: 学院“我心目中的好老师”评选活动圆满结束
date: 2013-05-29
content: 
<div class="Section1">
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; WIDOWS: 0; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; MARGIN: 0pt; ORPHANS: 0"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt"></span> </p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; WIDOWS: 0; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; MARGIN: 0pt; ORPHANS: 0"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">在全院学生的大力支持下，经过近一个月的评选，学院</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">“</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">我心目中的好老师</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">”</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">评选活动圆满结束。本次评选活动以调查问卷的形式在学院大一、大二及大三所有班级共</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">715</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">名学生中发放调查问卷，收回</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">594</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">张，有效问卷</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">586</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">张，回收率达</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">83%</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">，有效表格比例为</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">98%</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; WIDOWS: 0; TEXT-INDENT: 28pt; MARGIN: 0pt; ORPHANS: 0"><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">经过对调查问卷的准确计算和统计，郭江凌</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">、张新征及</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">罗勇</span><span style="FONT-FAMILY: '宋体'; FONT-SIZE: 14pt">老师获得总分前三名，获评为学生最受欢迎老师。</span></p></div>
